Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NCV Learning Platform collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information we may collect

  • Account information such as username and login details

  • Basic technical information such as browser type, pages visited, and device information

How information is used

  • To provide access to educational resources
  • To manage user accounts and platform security
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To monitor learning progress within the platform

Cookies and advertising

NCV Learning Platform may use cookies and similar technologies to improve the website experience. We may also use third-party services such as Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on visits to this and other websites.

Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings.

Data protection

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no online platform can guarantee complete security.

Contact
